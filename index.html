Bijay Thada Magar

Singer & Musician

My name is Bijay Thada Magar, born on August 23, 2001, to parents Ram Bahadur and Indramaya Magar. Although I come from a non-musical family, I was drawn to music from an early age. My parents, both strict teachers, still supported my interest and gifted me a guitar on my 15th birthday—a turning point that ignited my musical journey. What began as a hobby soon evolved into a deep passion, especially after receiving positive feedback on cover songs I posted online.

I officially began my professional journey when I joined Symphony Band in 2019. That same year, I released my first successful single, "Tadha-Tadha" on Kripa Drishya Digital. In 2021, I participated in The Voice Nepal Season 3, where I was selected by Coach Raju Lama during the blind auditions and reached the Top 11. Performing emotionally powerful songs such as "Jaat" by Chewang Lama and other rock and semi-classical pieces, I gained recognition for my vocal range and stage presence.

My second song with the band, “A Call From Nature (Mitho Maya)”, received a lot of love from listeners, further solidifying our place in the Nepali music scene. Currently, I’m working on my first full-length album titled "BHAWANA", with three songs already recorded and more in progress.

Live Performances & Musical Experience

With over a decade of performing experience, I've had the privilege of sharing my music across various venues in Nepal and internationally. My journey began with classical training and has evolved to embrace multiple genres including traditional Nepali folk, contemporary pop, and fusion.

I've performed at prestigious venues including the Kathmandu Jazz Festival, Thamel Music Festival, and various cultural events across the country. My performances blend traditional instruments with modern arrangements, creating a unique sound that resonates with diverse audiences.

Teaching & Future Goals

Passing on musical knowledge has become an integral part of my journey. I currently teach vocal techniques and instrumental skills to students of all ages, focusing on both traditional Nepali music and contemporary styles.

My teaching philosophy emphasizes building a strong technical foundation while encouraging students to develop their unique musical voice. I believe in creating a supportive environment where creativity can flourish.

Looking ahead, I aim to establish a music school that bridges traditional Nepali musical heritage with global contemporary styles, creating opportunities for young musicians to explore and innovate while honoring our rich cultural traditions.

What's Next

I'm currently working on my upcoming album that explores the fusion of traditional Nepali folk instruments with contemporary arrangements. This project aims to showcase the rich musical heritage of Nepal while creating sounds that resonate with global audiences.

Additionally, I'm planning a nationwide tour to promote cultural exchange through music, with performances scheduled in major cities across Nepal. These events will feature collaborations with local musicians, creating unique musical experiences that celebrate our diverse cultural landscape.

Stay tuned for announcements about upcoming releases, tour dates, and special performances!
